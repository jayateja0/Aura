01 — ABOUT

We build machines that don't just compute the world — they move through it.

Founded2021, Palo Alto
FocusGeneral-purpose humanoid robotics
DeploymentManufacturing, logistics, field ops

Most robots are built for one task, in one place, under one set of conditions. AURA started from a different premise: the physical world is not a fixed set of instructions — it's ambiguous, cluttered, and constantly changing, the same way it is for a person walking into a room for the first time.

Our platform pairs a full-body sensorimotor system with a perception stack trained on millions of hours of real and simulated interaction, letting AURA-1 recover from the unexpected — a dropped object, an uneven surface, a task that changes mid-motion — without a human rewriting its instructions.

We're a team of roboticists, control theorists, and machine learning researchers who believe embodiment is the next frontier of intelligence — and that it should be engineered with the same rigor as the models that power it.
